---
title: "IVRC 2025 – Seed Stage Selection"
date: 2025-09-01
---

Selected for the **SEED STAGE** at the Interverse Virtual Reality Challenge (IVRC 2025) with the project *"Crowded Train Breaker"*, representing The University of Osaka.

The project was developed as part of an 8-member team and presented at the Virtual Reality Society of Japan Conference.
